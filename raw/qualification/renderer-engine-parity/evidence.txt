CONTROL: the divergence is tree-state, not target shape
-------------------------------------------------------
The engine-build-id probe linked against BOTH halves of the SAME cached
artifact — GhosttyKit.xcframework (what the Workspace app links) and
lib-vt (the shape sessiond compiles) — reports one id:

  cached GhosttyKit.xcframework/macos-arm64_x86_64/libghostty-internal.a   0d9070c4...
  cached lib-vt/arm64/libghostty-vt.a                                      0d9070c4...

So the two consumers agree whenever they are built from the same tree. They
diverged because sessiond compiles vendor/ghostty from source at build time
(native/sessiond/build.zig takes ghostty as a zig dependency) while the app
links the SHARED CACHED xcframework, whose key ghostty-<commit>-zig-<version>
omits the patch series. 689bc0a0 regenerated the patches without moving the
commit, so the key collided:

  toolchain lock       patchedTree 130bc9e7  patchSeries a7dd844d
  cached artifact      patchedTree d92dc8fe  patchSeries ddeaf792

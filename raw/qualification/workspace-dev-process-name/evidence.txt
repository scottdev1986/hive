     `HiveWorkspace-<hash>`: the linker signs before the rename. This is NOT a

  2. The staged dev app under .dev/ (`make build && make run`) is produced by the

     to `make workspace`. Someone who deliberately hand-launches the stray
     binary still gets the ambiguous name. Closing that completely would mean
     renaming the SwiftPM product, which reaches the release build.
